# Life Chapters – AI Storybook Generator

Welcome to **Life Chapters**, an AI-powered demo application designed to help you create a personal storybook based on the people who have influenced your life.

## 🚀 Features
- **Input Form:** Easily add influential people, their roles, and your favorite memories.
- **AI Story Generation:** Generate heartfelt chapters in various styles (Casual, Poetic, Inspirational).
- **Deep Dive (Multi-Chaper Mode):** Transform a long, single-person story into a beautifully structured multi-chapter narrative.
- **Storybook Assembly:** View your chapters in a premium, glassmorphic reader with navigation.
- **Visualizations:** Explore your influences through an interactive timeline and relationship map.
- **Export:** Save your storybook as a PDF for sharing.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express, SQLite (via `sql.js`)
- **Frontend:** Vanilla HTML5, CSS3 (Glassmorphism), JavaScript
- **Security:** bcryptjs for hashed passwords, express-session for secure login
